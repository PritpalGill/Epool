Frequently Asked Questions

For busy people, who want to save money and time, Epool app makes it easy and fast to connect with other carpoolers. Unlike craigslist & facebook groups our product is a streamlined solution, with automatic user matching, and more.
Make sure to enter accurate details about your trip in Create a Pool page, including precise pickup and dropoff locations for passengers. Use the route name field to name your trip.
Epool recommends a 10 cents per km price per seat on trips. However, the price can be determined between passenger and driver.
Uber or Lyft drivers are employed by Uber or Lyft and earn a wage to drive people on demand. Epool drivers fill empty seats in their cars on existing journeys to cover the costs of their trip, and they are already driving somewhere. Uber or Lyft operates inside the city in real-time, much like a taxi. Epool helps people organize medium to long-distance trips between cities, usually a few days in advance.
